2023 Annual Report

Fiscal Year Ended December 31, 2023

Letter to Shareholders

Fiscal 2023 was a record year for Meridian Robotics, driven by strong
demand across all business segments. We expanded our installed base of
industrial automation systems and deepened customer relationships in
logistics and manufacturing.

We enter fiscal 2024 with a robust order backlog and disciplined cost
management.
<<<PAGEBREAK>>>
Financial Highlights

Meridian Robotics reported total net sales of $42.5 billion in fiscal
2023, an increase of 8% from $39.4 billion in fiscal 2022.

Operating income rose to $9.1 billion, and diluted earnings per share
were $4.18.
<<<PAGEBREAK>>>
Management's Discussion and Analysis

Meridian's gross margin was 38.2% in fiscal 2023, compared with 37.0%
in the prior fiscal year. The improvement was driven by favorable
product mix and manufacturing efficiencies.

Research and development expense was $5.2 billion, or 12% of net sales.
